The 80th Birthday Train Trip

Wed 16th to Sun 20th October 2019

London - Turin - Venice

Take Eurostar from London to Paris in 2 hours 15 minutes, have lunch at the amazing Train Bleu restaurant inside Paris Gare de Lyon, then take the afternoon high-speed TGV from Paris to Turin, a comfortable and scenic journey through the French Alps, see the video & photos here, arriving in the evening. Stay overnight in Turin.

Next morning take a fast Frecciabianca train from Turin to Venice in just a few hours. This works equally well in either direction, see the London to Italy by train page for all you need to know.

Venice - Zurich - London

For the ultimate scenic ride between London & Venice, take Eurostar & a TGV-Lyria to Zurich on day 1. Then take the wonderful narrow-gauge Bernina Express through the Swiss Alps to Tirano with connections for Venice on day 2, see the Bernina Express page.
